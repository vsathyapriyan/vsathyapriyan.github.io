Speech enhancement using binary estimator selection applied to hearing aids with a remote microphone

The demos consist of monaural left signals + monaural right signals for different noise and SNRs, headphones are recommended for listening.

  • The monaural left+ right signals are generated using measured HRIRs from the Kayser Database [2]
  • The speech signals are taken from the Dantale II [3]
    • System 1: Noisy RM - Noisy remote microphone signals received at the HA (with a TDOA w.r.t the local HA mirophone signals.)
    • System 2: MWF Estimate - MWF estimate of the target signal, using only the local HA microphone sisngals at given SNR.
    • System 3: BESC Estimate - Proposed method, using the MWF estimate and the delayed RM signal- For complex STFT coefficients.
    • System 4: MSTSA Estimate - MSTSA estimate of the target signal, using only the local HA microphone sisngals at given SNR.
    • System 5: BESM Estimate - Proposed method, using the MSTSA estimate and the delayed RM signal- For magnitude STFT coefficients.
